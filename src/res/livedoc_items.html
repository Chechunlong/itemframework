Projects and Items

Start by opening a project (.tpro file) from the filesystem (using the "Open Project" Button) or creating a new one (using the "New Project" Button).

Once you have an open project you can drag "Items" out of the Item-Toolbox onto the project-scene.

  • You can only connect inputs and outputs of the same color.
  • An input can be connected to multiple outputs but not vice versa.
  • To connect two items click on a input and drag a line to the output (release the mouse).
  • To delete items or connections select them with your mouse and press the "delete"-key.
  • You can select multiple items by pressing ctrl or by drawing a rectangle (rubber band selection).
